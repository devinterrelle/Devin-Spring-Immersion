Welcome To Devin's Corner!

Dev's Corner is a place for people to really understand what it is high school teens go through on a day to day basis when it comes to leadership postions.

"It's a hard Knock life for us - Jay-Z"

We see you came to find out more!

Devin is a fresh 17 year old who has various leadership roles on his campus at Hercules High School. Devin is Vice President of his Class (2026). He was also been awarded with becoming ASB Tresurer for the 2025-2026 school year. He is apart of Climate Team where a group of at least 3 students team up with Admin and Support Staff to help give student advice on how to fix the campus. He is a representaitive for Hercules High Schools Black Student Union as well as a secretary for Unicef Club as well. Devin is also a team manager for basketball and a member of School Site council where we plan the schools budget and work on the SPSA. There's a lot more where this came from.

The Journey of Becoming Who I am

Becoming who I am for sure was not an easy journey. Defining your leadership skills is just as hard as finding them. The easiest part is maintain your leadership status.. at least in my opinion.
